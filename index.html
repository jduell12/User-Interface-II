Jessica Duell

I am a Lambda Student working toward becoming a functional full-stack developer. I first got interested in programming when I was helping a friend with his free bootcamp experience. Once I got a taste of programming I fell in love and started to try and dabble with it in my free time. Even with the amount of free material available online it become overwhelming to try and determine where to start.

I found Lambda School when doing research to find guidance in my journey. Lambda's concept of teaching you and you pay them later made this an ideal situation. While learning at Lambda I love the community it inspires in not just each individual cohort but in the overall Lambda community. I have talked with students in my cohort, students farther ahead in their Lambda journey and even Lambda alumni. The support found here is definitely inspiring and confirms that I made the right choice for myself.

I look forward to honing my full-stack developer skills and adding new tools. I can't wait to see what I can accomplish.
